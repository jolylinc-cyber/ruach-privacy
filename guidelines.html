Community Guidelines

Spirit · Wind · Breath

RUACH is a faith community built on grace, respect, and love. These guidelines exist to keep RUACH a safe, encouraging place for everyone on their spiritual journey. By using RUACH, you agree to follow them.

"Behind every username is a real person on their own faith journey. Extend grace freely."

🤝Our Shared Commitments

  • Treat every member with dignity, regardless of denomination, background, or belief.
  • Keep prayer requests honest, personal, and faith-focused.
  • Encourage one another. A kind word can change someone's whole day.
  • Assume the best in others. Disagree with grace, never with contempt.

🚫What's Not Allowed

  • No attacks on others' faith. Do not attack, mock, or demean other Christian traditions, denominations, or anyone's beliefs.
  • No harassment. Do not harass, threaten, bully, or intimidate other users.
  • No explicit content. Do not post explicit, violent, or sexually suggestive material.
  • No spam. Do not post spam, chain messages, scams, or unsolicited promotional content.
  • No impersonation. Do not pretend to be someone else or misrepresent a church or organization.
  • No illegal activity. Do not use RUACH to promote or engage in anything unlawful.

🚩Reporting & Moderation

If you see something that violates these guidelines, use the in-app Report button on any post or comment. You can also block any user to stop seeing their content.

Our team reviews reports and may remove content or take action on accounts. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or a permanent ban without refund.

🙏Prayer & Encouragement

The prayer wall and groups are sacred spaces. Share authentically, lift others up, and honor the trust people place in this community when they share their burdens. You may post anonymously if you wish to protect your privacy.

💙A Note on Care

RUACH is not a crisis service or a substitute for professional mental health care. If you or someone in the community is in crisis, please reach out to a licensed professional or your local emergency services. In the U.S., you can call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) at any time.
