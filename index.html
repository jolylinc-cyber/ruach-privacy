RUACH

Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 25, 2026

RUACH ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the RUACH mobile application ("App"). By using RUACH, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, banner photo, denomination, bio/favorite verse, and public/private setting you choose to provide.
  • Church Information: If registering as a church, we collect church name, location, pastor name, contact information, website, and EIN number.
  • User Content: Prayer requests, encouragements, notes on Bible verses, bookmarked verses, and prayer group messages you create within the App.
  • Prayer Group Data: Group names, descriptions, invite codes, member lists, and messages sent within prayer groups you create or join.
  • AI Interaction Data: We track the number of AI feature uses per account for billing and limit enforcement purposes. We do NOT store the content of your conversations with the AI Bible Chat, Historical Info, or Modern Context features. Your AI conversation text is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription plan status (free, monthly, premium) and daily AI usage counts stored locally on your device and in our database.
  • Usage Data: App activity such as daily login streaks, reading history, and community interactions.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for technical support and crash reporting purposes.
  • Approximate Location: Optional, only when using the church finder feature. We use your approximate location to find nearby churches. We do not store your precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the RUACH App and its features
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account session
  • Enable community features including the prayer wall, church profiles, and prayer groups
  • Enforce AI usage limits for free and premium subscription tiers
  • Process donations to churches through our payment partner Stripe
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Respond to reports of inappropriate content
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. AI-Powered Features

RUACH uses Anthropic's Claude AI (model: claude-haiku-4-5) to power three features: Historical Info, Modern Context, and Bible Chat.

What we send to Anthropic: When you use an AI feature, the Bible passage you are reading and your typed question or request are transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. This transmission happens in real time and is not stored by RUACH.

What we do NOT store: The content of your AI conversations — your questions and the AI's responses — are not stored in our database. They exist only in your device's memory during your current app session and are cleared when you close the app.

What we DO track: We store a numeric count of how many AI calls your account has made, used to enforce the free tier limit (5 total uses) and the premium daily limit (100 uses per day). This count is associated with your account.

Anthropic's Privacy Policy: Your AI interactions are also subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

4. Anonymous Posts

RUACH allows users to post prayer requests and encouragements anonymously. When you choose to post anonymously, your name and profile photo are hidden from other users. For moderation and safety purposes, we internally retain a record of the original author's user ID. Your identity will never be revealed to other users for anonymous posts.

5. Prayer Groups

Prayer groups are private by design. Only users who have been given your group's unique invite code can join and view the group's messages. Group messages, member lists, and group names are stored in our database and are accessible only to current group members. When you delete a group or leave a group, your associated data is removed accordingly.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for database storage and authentication services. Your account data, profile, prayer posts, and group data are stored on Supabase servers. Supabase is bound by its own privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic: Bible passage text and your AI queries are transmitted to Anthropic for processing. See Section 3 above.
  • Stripe: Payment information for church donations and subscriptions is processed by Stripe. RUACH does not store your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Apple: Subscription billing is managed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to those transactions.
  • YouTube: Church live streaming is powered by YouTube embeds. YouTube's privacy policy applies when viewing streams.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time directly within the App:

Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account

Deleting your account will immediately remove all locally stored data from your device including bookmarks, notes, settings, and streak history. A request to delete your server-side data (profile, prayer posts, group memberships) will be processed within 30 days.

You may also request account deletion by emailing ruachbibleapp@gmail.com.

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

8. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption and row-level security policies. Passwords are never stored in plain text — authentication is handled entirely by Supabase Auth. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Profile photos and banner images are stored as references in our database. In the current version of the App, photos may be stored locally on your device. Future updates will transition to cloud-based storage.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (see Section 7)
  • Opt out of future communications from us
  • Data portability — request a copy of your data in a portable format

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ruachbibleapp@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

RUACH is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users between 13 and 17 must have parental consent to use the App. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at ruachbibleapp@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

11. User Generated Content

Prayer requests and encouragements you post publicly are visible to other RUACH users. You are responsible for the content you post. RUACH reserves the right to remove content that violates our Community Guidelines. Content that receives 3 or more reports from different users will be automatically hidden pending review. Prayer requests automatically expire after 7 days.

12. Subscriptions and Billing

RUACH offers optional premium subscriptions ($3.99/month or $24.99/year) processed through Apple's App Store. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. We store your subscription status (free or premium) in our database to enforce feature access. We do not store your payment card information — all billing is handled by Apple.

13. Donations and Financial Data

All church donation transactions are processed by Stripe. RUACH does not store credit card numbers or bank account information. Churches must provide a valid EIN number to receive donations. All donations are final and non-refundable through the App. For refund requests, contact the receiving church directly.

14. Offline Mode

RUACH includes an offline mode that allows Bible reading, bookmarks, and notes to function without an internet connection. In offline mode, no data is transmitted to our servers or third-party services. AI features, prayer wall, community features, and giving features are disabled in offline mode.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page and through a notice in the App. Continued use of RUACH after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
