Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 23, 2026

RUACH ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the RUACH mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password (encrypted).
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, banner photo, denomination, and favorite verse you choose to provide.
  • Church Information: If registering as a church, we collect church name, location, pastor name, contact information, website, and EIN number.
  • User Content: Prayer requests, encouragements, notes on Bible verses, and bookmarked verses you create within the app.
  • Usage Data: App activity such as daily login streaks, verses read, and prayers made.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for technical support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the RUACH app and its features
  • Enable community features such as the prayer wall and church profiles
  • Process donations to churches through our payment partner Stripe
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Respond to reports of inappropriate content

3. Anonymous Posts

RUACH allows users to post prayer requests and encouragements anonymously. When you choose to post anonymously, your name and profile photo are hidden from other users. However, for moderation and safety purposes, we internally retain a record of the original author. Anonymous posts will never have your identity revealed to other users.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database and authentication services, Stripe for payment processing, and Anthropic for AI-powered features. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.
  • Church Donations: When you donate to a church, your payment information is processed by Stripe. RUACH does not store your payment card details.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

To exercise these rights, contact us at ruachbibleapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

RUACH is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. User Generated Content

Prayer requests and encouragements you post publicly are visible to other RUACH users. You are responsible for the content you post. RUACH reserves the right to remove content that violates our Community Guidelines. Content that receives 3 or more reports from different users will be automatically hidden pending review.

9. Donations and Financial Data

All financial transactions are processed by Stripe. RUACH does not store credit card numbers or bank account information. Churches must provide a valid EIN number to receive donations. All donations are final and non-refundable through the app. For refund requests, contact the receiving church directly.

10. Third Party Services

RUACH integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic Claude — AI-powered Bible context features
  • YouTube — church live streaming
  • Apple Calendar — event saving (optional)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of RUACH after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
