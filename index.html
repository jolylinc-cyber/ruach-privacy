RUACH

Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 12, 2026

RUACH ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the RUACH mobile application ("App"). By using RUACH, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, banner photo, denomination, bio/favorite verse, home church, and public/private setting you choose to provide.
  • Church Information: If registering as a church, we collect church name, location, pastor name, contact information, phone number, email address, website, year founded, and EIN number (used only for 501(c)3 verification — never displayed publicly).
  • User Content: Prayer requests, encouragements, notes on Bible verses, bookmarked verses, shared verses, prayer group messages, and church posts you create within the App.
  • Prayer Group Data: Group names, descriptions, invite codes, member lists, and messages sent within prayer groups you create or join.
  • Achievement Data: Reading streak, prayers posted, encouragements given, and prayers prayed for others — stored as permanent running totals that never decrease.
  • Privacy Settings: Your choices to hide stats, following/followers counts, achievements, or set your profile to private — stored in our database and applied across all devices.
  • AI Interaction Data: We track the number of AI feature uses per account for limit enforcement. We do NOT store the content of your AI conversations. Your AI conversation text is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription plan status and usage counts stored in our database.
  • Usage Data: App activity such as daily login streaks, reading history, and community interactions.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for technical support and crash reporting purposes.
  • Approximate Location: Optional, only when using the church finder feature. We use your approximate location to find nearby churches. We do not store your precise location on our servers.
  • Contact Information (Optional): If you choose to add your phone number for contact syncing, we store only a one-way hashed code derived from your number — your actual phone number is never stored on our servers.
  • Notification Preferences: Your per-church notification settings and general notification preferences stored in our database.
  • Aggregate Usage Data: We analyze bookmark data across all users in aggregate — never individually identified — to power the Trending Verses feature in the Community tab. No personal information is exposed through this analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the RUACH App and its features
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account session
  • Enable community features including the prayer wall, church profiles, and prayer groups
  • Send bell notifications for prayers, encouragements, church posts, and follower activity
  • Display achievement badges based on your activity
  • Apply your privacy settings across all views of your profile
  • Allow contact syncing to help you find friends who are on RUACH
  • Enforce AI usage limits for free and premium subscription tiers
  • Process donations to churches through our payment partner Stripe
  • Send password reset and email change confirmation emails when requested
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Power the Trending Verses feature using aggregate, anonymized bookmark data
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Privacy Controls

RUACH gives you full control over what others can see on your profile:

  • Private Profile: Your profile is completely hidden from Community search. No one can find or follow you.
  • Hide Activity Stats: Hides your encouraged and prayer counts from other users.
  • Hide Following/Followers: Hides your follow counts and lists. Others cannot tap to see who you follow or who follows you.
  • Hide Achievements: Hides your achievement badges from other users' views while still showing them to you.
  • Anonymous Posts: Prayer requests and encouragements posted anonymously never display your name or photo to any other user.
  • Private Notes: Notes you write on Bible verses are stored securely on our servers and synced across your devices. They are private and never shared with anyone or visible on your public profile.

4. AI-Powered Features

RUACH uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power Historical Info, Modern Context, and Bible Chat features.

What we send to Anthropic: When you use an AI feature, the Bible passage you are reading and your typed question are transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. This transmission happens in real time and is not stored by RUACH.

What we do NOT store: The content of your AI conversations — your questions and the AI's responses — are not stored in our database.

What we DO track: We store a numeric count of how many AI calls your account has made, used to enforce usage limits.

Anthropic's Privacy Policy: Your AI interactions are also subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Contact Syncing

RUACH offers an optional contact sync feature to help you find friends who are also on RUACH.

  • Contact syncing only occurs when you explicitly tap "Sync Phone Contacts" in Settings
  • Your contacts' phone numbers are converted to private one-way codes (hashes) entirely on your device
  • Raw phone numbers from your contacts are never transmitted to our servers
  • Only the hashed codes are compared against our database
  • Adding your own phone number to be findable by contacts is entirely optional
  • You can remove your phone hash at any time by contacting us

6. Anonymous Posts

RUACH allows users to post prayer requests and encouragements anonymously. When you choose to post anonymously, your name and profile photo are hidden from all other users. For moderation and safety purposes, we internally retain a record of the original author's user ID. Your identity will never be revealed to other users for anonymous posts.

7. Church Profiles & Notifications

Church profiles are public by default. Church admins can post announcements, sermons, and events visible to subscribers. Subscriber lists are visible only to church admins.

When you subscribe to a church, you can control notification preferences per church. Turning off notifications for a church prevents church post notifications from appearing in your bell — this preference is stored in our database.

EIN numbers provided during church signup are used solely for 501(c)3 verification and are never displayed on public church profiles.

8. Prayer Groups

Prayer groups are private by design. Only users who have been given your group's unique invite code can join and view the group's messages. Group messages, member lists, and group names are stored in our database and are accessible only to current group members.

9. Notifications

RUACH sends in-app bell notifications for:

  • Encouragements on your prayer requests
  • When others pray for your prayer requests
  • New prayer requests from people you follow
  • Church announcements, sermons, and events from churches you subscribe to

You can dismiss notifications permanently. Dismissed notifications are stored locally on your device and will not reappear after logging out and back in.

10. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Supabase: Database storage and authentication. supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic: AI query processing. anthropic.com/privacy
  • Stripe: Church donation and subscription payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • Apple: In-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Expo/EAS: App delivery and build platform.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

11. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time directly within the App:

Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account

Deleting your account will immediately remove all locally stored data from your device and permanently delete all server-side data including bookmarks, notes, prayer history, streak history, and settings. Server-side data (profile, prayer posts, group memberships, notifications, church subscriptions) will be permanently deleted from our servers.

You may also request account deletion by emailing ruachbibleapp@gmail.com.

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

12. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption and row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Phone numbers are stored only as one-way hashed codes. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Data stored server-side includes bookmarks, notes, reading streak, prayer history, and account preferences — this allows your data to sync seamlessly across all your devices.

13. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Control your profile visibility through privacy settings
  • Opt out of contact syncing at any time
  • Data portability — request a copy of your data in a portable format

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ruachbibleapp@gmail.com.

14. Children's Privacy

RUACH is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users between 13 and 17 must have parental consent to use the App. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at ruachbibleapp@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

15. User Generated Content

Prayer requests and encouragements you post publicly are visible to other RUACH users based on your privacy settings. You are responsible for the content you post. RUACH reserves the right to remove content that violates our Community Guidelines. Content that receives 3 or more reports will be automatically hidden pending review. Prayer requests automatically expire after 7 days — however your achievement counts for posting prayers are permanent and never decrease.

16. Subscriptions and Billing

RUACH offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. We store your subscription status in our database to enforce feature access. We do not store your payment card information — all billing is handled by Apple.

17. Donations and Financial Data

All church donation transactions are processed by Stripe. RUACH does not store credit card numbers or bank account information. Churches must provide a valid EIN number to receive donations through the App. Note that church donations processed through Stripe are separate from Apple In-App Purchases and are not subject to Apple's payment policies.

18. Offline Mode

RUACH includes an offline mode that allows Bible reading to function without an internet connection. In offline mode, no data is transmitted to our servers or third-party services. AI features, prayer wall, community features, and giving features are disabled in offline mode. Bookmarks and notes created while offline will sync to our servers when your connection is restored.
